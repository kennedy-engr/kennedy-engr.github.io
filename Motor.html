Project Overview

This project is a simple motor driver circuit with 2 specifications in mind. The motor is
reversible and the speed of the motor is controlled by the duty cycle of the input voltage.

Hardware Materials

  • x1 STM32 Nucleo-G474RE Microcontroller
  • x2 HCPL-9030 Digital Isolators
  • x4 100nF Decoupling Capacitors
  • x4 Switches
  • x4 Protection Diodes
  • x1 5V to 20V Motor

Microcontroller(MCU) Peripherals

  • x6 GPIO Ports
  • x2 PWM Signals
  • x1 3.3v Line
